Traded vs Non-Traded: Structural Correlation Study

Universe split derived from V_trader_959k_submission.py · 3 days · 30,000 timestamps · 50 products

Headline findings

  • The PEBBLES family is a deterministic basket invariant — PEBBLES_L + PEBBLES_M + PEBBLES_S + PEBBLES_XL + PEBBLES_XS ≈ 50,000 with std/mean ≈ 5.6e-5. OLS gives PEBBLES_XL = 50,000 - 1.0000 · (M+S+L+XS) with residual std ≈ 2.8 chips. The bot trades XL/S/L but explicitly disables M and XS — leaving the basket arb on the table.
  • Apart from PEBBLES, no Non-Traded asset has a meaningful relationship with anything. Of 153 within-NT pairs, max |r| = 0.022. Of 576 cross-group pairs, only the two PEBBLES_XL pairings exceed |r| > 0.05.
  • No leading indicators. Lead-lag at ±1..±5 ticks for every PEBBLES pair collapses to |r| < 0.02. The basket relationship is purely contemporaneous.
  • SNACKPACK benchmark: similar basket structure with stronger pairwise return correlations (up to ±0.92) but a looser sum invariant (std/mean = 0.0038, vs 0.000056 for PEBBLES). The bot already trades all 5 SNACKPACK members.
