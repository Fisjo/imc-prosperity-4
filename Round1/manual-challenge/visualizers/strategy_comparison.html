Research math verification — where does ~13% come from?
Teammate's optimizer finds Research% by maximizing ln(1+x)·(C−x) where C = remaining budget after Speed. At C≈52%, the calculus gives:
C (remaining)Optimal Research%Optimal Scale%Research output
The R≈13% comes from a large C being split very unevenly — Scale wins because it multiplies linearly against a log-curve that flattens fast.
