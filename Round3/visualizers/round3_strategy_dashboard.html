Key risk factors
Gamma risk
Near-ATM options (5200–5400) have highest gamma. Large VEV moves create P&L drag if hedge rebalancing lags.
Theta decay benefit
Short options collect theta: VEV_5300 loses ~5.8/day, VEV_5400 ~3.8/day. Flat market = profit from time decay.
VEV hedge capacity
VEV limit is 200. If options delta exceeds ±200, hedge is capped. Prioritise lower-delta vouchers or reduce option size.
Expiry risk (TTE→0)
As TTE → 0 in rounds 4–7, delta collapses fast for OTM options. Reduce OTM positions aggressively in later rounds.
